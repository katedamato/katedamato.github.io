About

I am a Master of Science in Data Science candidate at the University of Virginia (GPA: 4.0) and a Portfolio Manager/Quantitative Analyst at Freddie Mac. I specialize in leveraging Python, SQL, and R to extract insights from large-scale datasets and build predictive economic models.

My technical expertise spans cloud-based data platforms like Databricks and AWS Sagemaker, as well as advanced statistical modeling and BI tools like Tableau and PowerBI. I am committed to transforming complex data into evidence-based policy and business recommendations.
